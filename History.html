HISTORY OF OMETO FAMILY

ORIGIN

The origin of Ometo family stated since 1880s by her forefather by the name Salihu. He is handsome,beautiful intelegent and brilliant man that was born at one village called Okaba in Kogi State-Nigeria.

THE NAME OMETO

The name was found the great man of Okaga Village Pa Salihu. The only man that has oppotunity to attain school in the entire village went to school. When he came to school newly, there were many people with the name salihu which lead people to confusion. Hence Pa Salihu decided to name himself Ometo which litraly mean the new arrive.

CONTRIBUTION

Pa Salihu Ometo and his children has contribute greatly to the
development of people in religous, economically, social, and political aspect. They are up to date remembern as great by the people of Okaba village and kogi-state at large.
